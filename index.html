  • Freedom Trail

    The Freedom Trail is a walkway with red brick lines on a road in central Boston, Massachusetts, USA. It spans about 2.5-miles from Boston Common to the Bunker Hill Monument in Charlestown, allowing you to explore 16 of the city's major historical attractions.

  • Harvard University

    Harvard University is a comprehensive private university located in Cambridge, near Boston, Massachusetts, USA. It is said that good luck comes when you touch the shoes of the statue, which is said to be Harvard in the university.

  • Fenway Park

    Fenway Park is a baseball field in Boston, Massachusetts, USA. It is famous as the home stadium of the MLB Boston Red Sox.
